Support & Terms

Community support is offered through our Discord Server.

The policy below applies to all apps made by ListenUp Ai Ao including Tour Record and ListenUp mobile apps on all platforms. PLEASE read it carefully.

Personal information

We make all of our apps with your privacy in mind, so we do not collect personally identifiable information about you. In other words, unless you explicitly provide it for usefullness of our app, we do not secretly collect information such as your name, address, phone number or email address. We do capture precise geographic location, your audio input and photographs, or other parameters from your device that makes our app useful, but it is not stored in connection with your identifying information, and it is at your discretion that this data is shared. We do not share it with third parties unless forced to by authorities, and despite our best efforts, data breaches are always a possibility. It is your responsibility to use best practices to secure your own data.

Usage analytics

We want to make our product more useful for you, so we may use Analytics in our apps. It collects user behavior information in an anonymous form and transmits it to our servers. That means only app usage data is collected, no personally identifiable information. Then we can use our Analytics to create reports of how our app is being used. We use these reports to understand how to spend our development resources more efficiently and make our app better.

Changes

We will post any privacy policy changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policies, please post it to the support channel on our Discord Server.
